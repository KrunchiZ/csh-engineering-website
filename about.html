Our Story

Built on Experience

CSH Engineering Services & Trading was established with a focus on the repair and reconditioning of electronic controller boards for industrial air compressors. Over 25 years, we have built a reputation as a trusted specialist for major compressor brands including Ingersoll Rand, Kaeser, Sullair, Atlas Copco and more.

Alongside our core controller business, we provide packaging machinery solutions — servicing and modifying automatic packaging systems for the food, pharmaceutical and industrial sectors. Our metal fabrication arm specialises in laser cut metal enclosures, supplying FACP enclosures, intercom boxes and bespoke metalwork for the fire safety and various other industries.

Based in Puchong, Selangor, we serve industrial clients across Malaysia with fast turnaround times and practical, cost-effective solutions.
